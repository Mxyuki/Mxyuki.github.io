Dragon in Disguise

Chapter 1 : A Dragon's Boredom

Ryuu was a dragon, and she had been alive for centuries. In all that time, she had seen and experienced everything there was to see and experience. She had flown to the highest peaks and dived to the deepest depths of the oceans. She had battled other dragons and defeated countless foes. She had amassed a hoard of treasure beyond compare, and she had enjoyed every moment of it.

But now, Ryuu was bored. She had done it all, and nothing seemed new or exciting anymore. She longed for something different, something that would make her heart race with anticipation.

She spent her days in her lair, sleeping and waiting for something to happen. She occasionally went out to hunt, but even that had lost its appeal. The prey was easy to catch, and the challenge was nonexistent.

Ryuu was restless, and she knew she had to find a way to break the monotony of her life. She scoured the ancient tomes in her hoard, looking for anything that could help her. She found spells that could summon mythical beasts and conjure powerful storms, but she had done all those things before.

Then, she came across a spell that caught her attention. It was a transformation spell that could turn her into a human girl with dragon horns. Ryuu had never heard of such a thing, but the idea intrigued her.

She read the spell over and over again, trying to understand how it worked. After a few days of studying, she finally felt confident enough to try it.

She cleared a space in her lair and began to chant the spell. She focused her energy on the incantation, channeling her magic into the transformation. As she chanted, her body began to change. Her scales receded, and her wings vanished. In their place, soft skin formed, and small horns sprouted from her forehead.

Ryuu was stunned. She had never seen herself like this before. She looked down at her hands and marveled at how delicate they were. She stood up and took a few steps, feeling the ground beneath her feet. She stumbled a bit, unused to the sensation of walking.

But she soon found her balance and began to walk around her lair, feeling the strange new body she now inhabited. She could feel her heart racing with excitement. This was something new and different, and she was eager to explore.

Ryuu decided that she would use her new form to explore the human world. She knew very little about humans, except that they were weak and fragile compared to dragons. But she was curious, and she wanted to see what they were like up close.

With her newfound form, Ryuu set off on a journey that would change her life forever.
