Dragon in Disguise

Description :

Ryuu, a dragon, becomes bored of her mundane life and decides to transform into a cute human girl with dragon horns to explore the human world. In her new form, she discovers the joys of human food and develops an insatiable appetite for it. She meets Tatsuo, a kind-hearted human who shows her kindness, and the two become friends. However, Ryuu's hunger for human food threatens to consume her, and she must learn to control it before it's too late. Along the way, she navigates the challenges of human society and begins to understand the true meaning of friendship and love.
