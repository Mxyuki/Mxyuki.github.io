Chapter 1 : The Summoning

Yuji had never believed in magic. He had always thought that the world was a mundane and predictable place, governed by the laws of science and reason. But all of that changed when he was suddenly whisked away to another world by a powerful mage.

The summoning had been a shock to Yuji's system. He had felt a sensation like nothing he had ever experienced before – as if his very soul was being torn from his body. When the sensation faded and he opened his eyes, he found himself standing in the middle of a strange, otherworldly chamber.

At first, Yuji was too disoriented to take in his surroundings. But as his senses gradually returned to him, he began to notice the details of the chamber: the intricate magical symbols etched into the walls, the flickering torches that provided the only source of light, and the imposing figure of the mage who stood before him.

The mage was an imposing figure, tall and broad-shouldered, with a thick beard and piercing eyes. He regarded Yuji with a mixture of curiosity and disdain.

"So," he said, in a voice that rumbled like thunder. "You are the one I have summoned."

Yuji didn't know what to say. He had so many questions – how had he been summoned? Where was he? And most importantly, how was he going to get back home?

But before he could voice any of these concerns, something strange began to happen to him. He felt a sudden, intense pain in his chest, as if he was being ripped apart from the inside. He clutched at his chest, gasping for air, as his body began to change.

His hair grew longer and more lustrous, his features softened and became more delicate, and his clothes transformed into a flowing gown. Yuji felt his body growing lighter and more ethereal, until he looked down and saw that he had become…a girl.

Yuji was stunned. How had this happened? Was this some kind of bizarre side effect of the summoning?

The mage regarded Yuji with a hint of amusement. "It seems I have made a slight miscalculation in my spell," he said, almost apologetically. "But no matter. You will still serve my purposes just fine."

Yuji didn't like the sound of that. He had a sinking feeling that he was in for a lot of trouble. But before he could say anything, the mage began to chant an incantation, and a bright light enveloped Yuji's body.

The next thing Yuji knew, he was falling through space and time, hurtling towards a new world that he had never seen before. As he fell, he felt a strange, tingly sensation in his fingertips, as if he was being infused with a powerful new energy.

When he landed, Yuji looked around him, dazed and confused. He saw that he was in a lush, green forest, filled with trees and plants that he had never seen before. The air was thick with the scent of wildflowers and pine needles, and the sky was a deep shade of blue.

But as Yuji looked around, he realized that he wasn't alone. A group of strange, humanoid creatures had gathered around him, chattering and pointing at him with curious expressions on their faces.

Yuji had no idea what was going on, or what was going to happen to him. But one thing was for sure – his life was never going to be the same again.
